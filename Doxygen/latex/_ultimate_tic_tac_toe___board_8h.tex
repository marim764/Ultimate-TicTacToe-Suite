\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Defines the board class for Ultimate Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe game. 

Ultimate Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe is played on a 9x9 board divided into 3x3 small boards. Players win small boards, and winning the main 3x3 of small boards wins the game.
