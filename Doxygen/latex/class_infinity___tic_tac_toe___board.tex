\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements Infinity Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe with disappearing moves. 

A 3x3 Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe variant where after every 3 moves, the oldest move on the board disappears. Players must achieve 3-\/in-\/a-\/row before their pieces vanish. Creates a dynamic, time-\/pressured gameplay experience.
