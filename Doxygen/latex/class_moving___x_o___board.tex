\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
4x4 Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe board where players start with 4 tokens each and move tokens. 

Rules implemented\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \doxylink{class_board}{Board} size\+: 4x4
\item Starting layout\+: Row 0\+: O X O X Row 1\+: . . . . Row 2\+: . . . . Row 3\+: X O X O
\item On a turn, a player picks one of his tokens and moves it to an adjacent (horizontal or vertical) empty square. No diagonal moves, no jumping.
\item Winning condition\+: first player to align THREE of their tokens in a row, column, or diagonal wins. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
