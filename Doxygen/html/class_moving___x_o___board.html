Detailed Description

4x4 Tic-Tac-Toe board where players start with 4 tokens each and move tokens.

Rules implemented:

  • Board size: 4x4
  • Starting layout: Row 0: O X O X Row 1: . . . . Row 2: . . . . Row 3: X O X O
  • On a turn, a player picks one of his tokens and moves it to an adjacent (horizontal or vertical) empty square. No diagonal moves, no jumping.
  • Winning condition: first player to align THREE of their tokens in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
